Overnight Oats

Looking for an easy to make and highly nutritional breakfast?

And to top it all you can make it with pretty much anything! So the taste is 100% guaranteed.

    Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Yogurt
  • Oats
  • Honey
  • Chia seeds
  • Cinnamon
  • Berries

    Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients (besides the fruit) in a jar, seal, and shake.
  2. Open the jar and fold in the fruit.
  3. Seal the jar and refrigerate overnight.
