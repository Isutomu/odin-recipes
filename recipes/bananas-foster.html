Bananas Foster

Sometimes you just want to eat a sweet treat, but made really fast without sacrificing the flavor.

This could also be another definition of Bananas Foster in the Oxford dictionary. Besides being delicious it looks the part too, making it perfect to sate your crave!

    Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Rum
  • Vanilla
  • Cinnamon
  • Bananas
  • Walnuts
  • Ice cream

    Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large, deep skillet over medium heat. Stir in brown sugar, rum, vanilla, and cinnamon; bring to a low boil.
  2. Place bananas and walnuts in the pan. Cook until bananas have softened, 1 to 2 minutes. Serve at once over vanilla ice cream.
